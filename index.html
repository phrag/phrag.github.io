About Me

Security has been in my DNA for over 25 years. I started with electrical engineering and Linux systems, grew through roles at IBM, BBC, AOL, TomTom, and Schuberg Philis, and have spent the last 7.5 years building security at SoundCloud from the ground up.

I believe the best security teams are the ones people want to work with. I've built a culture at SoundCloud where employees feel safe bringing security problems forward without fear of blame — because that's how you actually reduce risk.

Beyond work, I'm passionate about privacy, open source, and digital rights. I've run Tor nodes, organised OpSec workshops for activists, and contributed to projects that protect people's freedom online.

I've been on SoundCloud as a listener for 15 years — long before I joined the company. Music is a constant in my life, and protecting the platform that connects artists and fans is something I take personally.
